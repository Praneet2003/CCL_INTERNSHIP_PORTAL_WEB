CCL Certificate Assurance

Central Coalfields Limited assures that all eligible interns who successfully complete their internship program will receive an official Certificate of Completion.

This certificate serves as valid proof of industrial training and includes:

  • Intern’s Name and Department
  • Internship Duration
  • Project Title or Training Domain
  • Remarks or Evaluation by Mentor
  • Authorized Signature and Official Stamp

Eligibility Criteria for Certification

To be eligible for the certificate:

  • Intern must complete the full duration of the internship.
  • Submit required reports or project work.
  • Maintain discipline and regularity during training.

Digital Verification (Optional)

Where applicable, each certificate may contain a unique Certificate ID or QR code for online verification, ensuring authenticity and validation for academic or employment purposes.
